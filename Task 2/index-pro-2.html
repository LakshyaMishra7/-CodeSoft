About MINECRAFT!

Minecraft is a sandbox video game that lets players explore, build, and survive in a blocky world created by algorithms. It promotes creativity, crafting, and adventure through different modes, including survival, creative, and multiplayer. This game appeals to people of all ages.

Beginner's Guide!

Here is the way you can start your first Minecraft World:

  1. Gather resources (Like wood, stone, food)
  2. Build your first shelter before night time
  3. Craft tools and weapons(like pickaxe, sword)
  4. Explore caves, villages, and portals!
